Build a powerful, well-rounded chest.

BACK EXERCISES

Build width and thickness for a V-taper back.

Back — Full

BARBELL DEADLIFT

Hinge at hips, grip bar outside knees, drive through heels. Keep back neutral throughout.

BarbellCompound4×5-8
Back — Width

PULL-UPS

Grip bar wider than shoulders. Pull chest to bar, squeeze lats at top. Control the descent.

BodyweightCompound4×Max
Back — Thickness

BARBELL ROW

Bend at 45°, pull bar to lower chest. Squeeze shoulder blades together at the top.

BarbellCompound4×8-12

LEG EXERCISES

Complete lower body development — quads, hamstrings, and glutes.

Quads & Glutes

BARBELL BACK SQUAT

Bar on upper traps, feet shoulder-width. Descend until thighs parallel, drive up through heels.

BarbellCompound4×6-10
Quads

LEG PRESS

Feet shoulder-width on platform. Lower until 90° knee angle, press up without locking knees.

MachineBeginner4×10-15
Glutes & Balance

WALKING LUNGES

Step forward, lower back knee to floor, push through front heel. Alternate legs continuously.

DumbbellsFunctional3×12/leg

SHOULDERS & ARMS

Build boulder shoulders and sleeve-busting arms.

Shoulders

OVERHEAD PRESS

Press barbell from shoulders to overhead lockout. Core tight, squeeze glutes for stability.

BarbellCompound4×6-10
Side Delts

LATERAL RAISES

Raise dumbbells to sides until parallel with floor. Lead with elbows, controlled descent.

DumbbellsIsolation4×12-15
Biceps

BARBELL BICEP CURL

Elbows pinned to sides. Curl bar up, squeeze at top, slow 3-second negative on the way down.

BarbellIsolation3×10-12
