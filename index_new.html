No! SelfieGym works for all fitness levels. Whether you're a complete beginner or an advanced lifter, our AI adapts programs specifically to your level and goals. We have comprehensive video tutorials for every exercise.

Our AI learns from your fitness data, performance, recovery, and feedback. It continuously adapts your workouts, adjusts intensity, and provides real-time guidance. Think of it like having a world-class trainer studying you 24/7.

Yes, absolutely. No contracts, no hidden fees, no hassle. You can cancel your subscription anytime from your account settings. We're confident you'll love SelfieGym and want to stay.
