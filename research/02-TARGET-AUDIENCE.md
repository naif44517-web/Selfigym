### Segment 1: Young Professionals (25-35)
**Demographics:**
- Age: 25-35
- Gender: Both (slight male skew 55/45)
- Income: Middle to upper-middle (15,000+ SAR/month)
- Location: Riyadh, Jeddah, Dammam
- Education: University degree

**Psychographics:**
- Health-conscious but time-poor
- Tech-savvy, use multiple apps daily
- Value convenience over price
- Follow fitness influencers
- Prefer working out at home or flexible gym schedules

- Expensive gym memberships
- Inconsistent motivation
- Don't know proper form
- Confused about nutrition

**How SelfieGym Helps:**
- 24/7 AI trainer fits their schedule
- Cheaper than personal trainer
- Daily reminders and motivation
- Form guidance with videos
- Personalized meal plans

---

### Segment 2: Fitness Beginners (18-30)
**Demographics:**
- Age: 18-30
- Gender: Both
- Income: Entry-level (5,000-15,000 SAR/month)
- Location: Urban Saudi Arabia
- Education: High school to university

**Psychographics:**
- Want to get fit but don't know where to start
- Intimidated by gyms
- Active on social media (Instagram, TikTok, Snapchat)
- Price-sensitive
- Influenced by peer recommendations

**Pain Points:**
- Overwhelmed by fitness information
- Don't know what exercises to do
- Embarrassed to ask questions
- Can't afford personal trainer
- Need guidance on diet

**How SelfieGym Helps:**
- Friendly AI that answers any question
- Step-by-step beginner programs
- No judgment, private coaching
- Free tier to start
- Arabic language support

---

### Segment 3: Home Workout Enthusiasts (25-45)
**Demographics:**
- Age: 25-45
- Gender: 60% female, 40% male
- Income: Variable
- Location: Anywhere in Saudi Arabia
- Family status: Often married with children

**Psychographics:**
- Prefer working out at home
- May have family responsibilities
- Consistent but need variety
- Value privacy
- Looking for efficient workouts

**Pain Points:**
- Bored with YouTube workouts
- Need equipment-free options
- Time constraints (30 min or less)
- No one to track progress
- Need accountability

**How SelfieGym Helps:**
- Home workout programs (no equipment)
- Short, effective routines
- Progress tracking
- Smart reminders
- Variety of workouts

---

### Segment 4: Women-Only Fitness (18-40)
**Demographics:**
- Age: 18-40
- Gender: Female
- Location: Saudi Arabia
- Cultural consideration: Prefer women-focused content

**Psychographics:**
- Want to exercise privately
- Interested in toning, not bulking
- Value modesty in fitness content
- Active on WhatsApp and Instagram
- Trust female influencer recommendations

**Pain Points:**
- Many gym photos show men
- Uncomfortable with co-ed gyms
- Hard to find culturally appropriate content
- Need support in Arabic

**How SelfieGym Helps:**
- Women's toning programs
- Private, AI-only interaction
- Culturally appropriate content
- Home workouts for privacy
- Arabic support
