### 2. Influencer Marketing

#### Tier 1: Micro-Influencers (10K-50K followers)
- **Cost:** 500-2,000 SAR per post
- **Focus:** Authentic recommendations
- **Target:** 5-10 influencers in Month 1

**Types to Target:**
- Fitness trainers (male & female)
- Health/wellness bloggers
- Lifestyle influencers
- Mommy bloggers (for women's market)

#### Tier 2: Mid-Tier Influencers (50K-200K)
- **Cost:** 2,000-10,000 SAR per post
- **Focus:** Broader reach, credibility
- **Target:** 2-3 influencers after initial traction

#### Partnership Structure:
```
Option A: Paid Post
- One-time fee for post/story
- Include promo code for tracking

Option B: Affiliate
- Free access to premium
- Commission on conversions (20-30%)
- Long-term relationship

Option C: Ambassador
- Monthly retainer
- Exclusive partnership
- Regular content creation
```
