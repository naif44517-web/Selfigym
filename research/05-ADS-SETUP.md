### Ad Creative Best Practices

#### Video Ads (Best Performing)
```
- Length: 15-30 seconds
- Hook in first 3 seconds
- Show app in action
- Add Arabic captions
- End with clear CTA
- Vertical format (9:16) for Stories/Reels
```

#### Carousel Ads
```
- 3-5 cards
- Each card = one benefit
- Consistent visual style
- Arabic text
- Final card = CTA
```
